FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before you get started.

How fast can I start accepting payments?
Most sellers are up and receiving payments within 60 seconds of signing up. Our automated KYC verification works in real time, so there's no waiting period for basic tiers.
When do I actually receive my money?
On the Starter plan, funds are settled within 3 business days. Pro users receive same-day payouts, and Enterprise users can access instant payouts to a linked debit card.
Is there a transaction fee on top of my plan cost?
Starter accounts have a 2.5% transaction fee. Pro and Enterprise accounts have a flat 0.8% fee — no surprise deductions, just a transparent cut that shrinks as you scale.
Can I use PaySalePro with my existing tools?
Yes. We offer native integrations with Shopify, WooCommerce, Zapier, Notion, HubSpot, and Slack. Pro and Enterprise plans also get access to our full REST API for custom integrations.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. PaySalePro is PCI DSS Level 1 certified and SOC 2 Type II audited. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We never store raw card numbers on our servers.
Can I cancel my plan at any time?
Yes — no lock-in, no cancellation fees. You can downgrade or cancel from your dashboard any time. If you cancel mid-billing cycle, you retain access until the end of the period.
